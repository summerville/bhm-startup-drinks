A simple concept:  A grassroots effort has sprung up in cities around the world where people gather around a bar to have a pint and discuss what they are working on, what they need help with and what they can do for each other. No presentations. No pitches. No sponsors or speakers, just drinks and startup talk.

Who should attend?  Existing or aspiring entrepreneurs of all types and ages. You can bring whomever you like; everyone is welcome!

When?  5:30 - 7:30 p.m. every fourth Tuesday of the month.

Next one up is Tuesday, January 27th, 2014.

Where?  Trim Tab Brewing (2721 5th Avenue South, Birmingham, AL 35233)
